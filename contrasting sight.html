CONTRASTING SIGHTS

Genesis 13/10

19/10/2023

"And Lot lifted his eyes, and beheld all the plain of Jordan, that it was well watered everywhere, before the Lord destroyed Sodom and Gomorrah, even as the garden of the Lord, like the land of Egypt, as thou comest unto Zoar."

Lot was about to make a choice that would forever determine his destiny and legacy. With his physical eyesight, Lot saw a well-watered plain of Jordan "And Lot lifted his eyes, and beheld all the plain of Jordan, that it was well watered everywhere"

With his physical eyesight, he saw the land of Sodom and Gomorrah as the garden of the Lord and the land of Egypt - _"Sodom and Gomorrah, even as the garden of the Lord, like the land of Egypt, as thou comest unto Zoar."

But from the divine eyesight, all the land that he saw was to be later destroyed by the Lord "before the Lord destroyed Sodom and Gomorrah"

Unfortunately for Lot, he was unaware of the impending destruction of Sodom and Gomorrah before relocating to Sodom.

He did not enquire of the Lord before migrating with his whole household into Sodom. Following his physical sense of sight, Lot suffered terrible losses of possessions and properties when Sodom and Gomorrah came under divine wrath, judgment, and destruction.

Lot's wife also ended up as a pillar of salt when she looked back from behind Lot and never reached the promised land of escape that God mercifully wanted for Lot!

Life is full of countless choices that you consciously and unconsciously make. The choices you made yesterday were what determined where you are today and the choices you are making today will determine your chances tomorrow. Major determinants of your choices are your physical senses and intelligence which are limited to your past experiences and present realities. The best physical senses and intelligence can not discern what God will do and what your tomorrow will be.

But with God, you can enjoy divine senses and intelligence to know all the intentions of God and what your tomorrow will be. But you must be humble enough to desire and enquire of the Lord to know his divine perspective of things before making any choice in life.

The choices you make over time will determine your destiny and legacy over time.

PRAYER

O lord fortify me with divine senses and intelligence to make choices that will grant me the most glorious destiny, legacy, and eternity in Jesus' name üôè.

YOUR CHOICES TODAY WILL EITHER MAKE OR BREAK YOU TOMORROW!
